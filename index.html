Rules of the game


The computer generates random cards, and each card contains a certain number of points.
Ace is 11; Jack, Lady and the King are 10, and the rest of the cards' signs correspond to the points they contain.
When the player has more than 21 points, the round is done, and the player loses 20$.
In case of 21 points (Black Jack), player's money is doubled.
